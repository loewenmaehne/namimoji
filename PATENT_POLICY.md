That copyright license grants no patent rights. This statement describes, separately, the editor's intent regarding the relevant patent rights. Copyright and patent are distinct legal instruments; implementers should consider both.

## 2. The patent

The editor is the inventor and applicant of **Dutch Patent Application NL 4001519**, filed 2026-04-20 at Octrooicentrum Nederland. Where this statement refers to the editor's patent rights, it means NL 4001519 together with the patents and applications descending from it — continuations, divisionals, and filings in other jurisdictions claiming its Paris Convention priority (its "patent family").

Under the registration-patent procedure of the Dutch Patent Act in force for applications filed on that date, a national patent is granted following a novelty search report, without substantive examination of novelty or inventive step. The application is laid open to public inspection approximately eighteen (18) months after filing; its status is verifiable through the Dutch patent register. Subsequent priority-claiming filings (PCT, EP, US, or other jurisdictions) may or may not be pursued as funding permits; the twelve-month Paris Convention priority window for such filings runs until on or about 2027-04-20.

**No assurance is given as to the grant, validity, enforceability, or scope of any patent, and any claims that issue may differ from the application as filed.** Implementers are responsible for their own patent due diligence, including as to third-party patents.

## 3. Licensing intent

When the full Patent Policy becomes operative with Specification v1.0, the editor intends to offer licenses to those claims of NL 4001519 and its patent family that are **necessary to implement the specification**, on the following basis:

- **Non-commercial and small-scale implementers** — individuals, academic and open-source projects, and small organizations: **royalty-free**.
- **Larger commercial implementers**: on reasonable terms, negotiated individually.

The terms "non-commercial" and "small-scale" indicate intended direction, not present eligibility; the full Patent Policy will define them, define precisely which patents are covered, and define how future family members and any later filings are treated. Nothing in this statement is itself a commitment as to claims not yet filed. This is an expression of intent, not a present grant.

No license application process exists yet; one will be published with the full Patent Policy at Specification v1.0. Until then, there is nothing to apply for.

The editor retains ownership of the copyright in this repository's CC BY 4.0 materials and of the patent rights described above, and reserves the right to assign or transfer that ownership to a successor of the editor's choosing — the editor's present intent being a non-profit foundation established to steward Namimoji. Arrangements for long-term continuity, including any copyright and patent assignment, will be set out in the full Patent Policy or an accompanying instrument published with Specification v1.0.
